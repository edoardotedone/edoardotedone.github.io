ORDINARY MUSES


30 March 2019
@Onomatopee
Eindhoven

A small publication as an ode to ordinary objects. Containing 55 word stories that are the results of a flash fiction workshop run at Onomatopee by Lara Chapman and me, as an initiative of the Master Department Design Curating and Writing at Design Academy Eindhoven, planned with the assistance of Tiiu Meiner. The booklet was written, designed and risoprinted during the Super Book Saturday, 30 March 2019 at Onomatopee, Eindhoven.
