BREAK THE CYCLE: A Non-linear Take


Despite the confidence with which I have observed and scrutinized the European rap video culture and the French suburbia, I wonder if I was truly able to put myseld in other people's shoes. So, rather than leave this austere sequende of thoughts alone, I'd like to trace my thoughts back to the mishmash of images I came across while tuning myself to the sensitivities of several artist; since I have the feeling that most of the ealy hazy impressions I've got, washed away in the process of refining thoughts into a rigidely scripted dissertation.

So, here is a non-linear take on the same story, made of several image archives and some matchin keywords as a guideline - in the hope to foster alternative views and endings to ‘my story’.
