MAJONEZË SHORTFILM

DESCRIPTION

Key VFX sequences were designed to enhance the visual narrative of Majonezë, a black & white short film (Canal+ Award, David di Donatello nomination). By animating elements from a photo archive and introducing a contrasting gold texture, a visually compelling moment was crafted, resonating deeply with the film's overall theme.

Client Lupin Film
Year 2024
Category VFX
