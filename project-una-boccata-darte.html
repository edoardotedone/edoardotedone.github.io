UNA BOCCATA D'ARTE

DESCRIPTION

For Una Boccata d’Arte 2024, a widespread contemporary art project across Italy, I created a detailed CGI environment in Unreal Engine for Agostino Quaranta's art intervention in Gioia Dei Marsi, Abruzzo. This digital environment was then printed onto large-scale banners, integrating seamlessly with the artist's work and the local landscape.

Client Agostino Quaranta & Threes Productions
Year 2023
Category Exhibition
